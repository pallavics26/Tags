A website is a collection of interlinked web pages that are hosted on a server and can be accessed through the internet using a web browser (like Chrome, Edge, or Firefox). When you type a website's address (URL) into your browser (for example, https://www.google.com), your browser requests information from the website's server, and the server responds by sending back the content (HTML, CSS, images, videos, etc.), which your browser displays.

What is blogging website?

A blogging website is a type of website that mainly focuses on publishing articles or posts in a chronological or categorized format. These posts are usually written by an individual or a group and often cover specific topics, opinions, news, tutorials, or experiences.
